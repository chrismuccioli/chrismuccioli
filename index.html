Chris Muccioli is a multi-disciplinary designer working in Brooklyn, NY.

He is a co-founder and creative director of quarterly record club & music journal Singles Club. Previously he led design at Jukely & has worked with the fine folks at Kickstarter, 160over90, & Post Typography. Currently available for new projects.
